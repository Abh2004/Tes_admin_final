OUR STORY

IIT KHARAGPUR
Technology Environment Society

"Drawing wisdom from Mahatma Gandhi's insightful words, 'The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others,' we are resolutely committed to forging an equitable and harmonious world that stands the test of time. The formidable repercussions of climate change imperil us all due to our heedless exploitation and human-induced degradation of precious natural resources. A staggering 24% of global fatalities stem from the consequences of environmental degradation, encompassing a distressing 28% of mortality among children under five. Consequently, immediate collective action from stakeholders is imperative to reinstate the well-being of both humanity and the ecosystem. Within the folds of the Technology Environment Society, we ardently labor to cultivate an equitable and sustainable society, meticulously addressing every facet of sustainabilityâ€”be it environmental, social, or economic. Yet, a recurrent fallacy persists whenever the topic of sustainability arises: the notion that economic progress and environmental preservation are incompatible. In truth, a delicate balance must be struck amongst these three pillars of sustainability. Our aspirations for this symbiotic synergy encompass the embracement of education, empowerment, and egalitarianism, fostering robust health and adaptability in our 'ONLY HOME.' Our Vision Embarking on a trajectory toward an impartial and sustainable society // Envisioning a future where equity and sustainability intertwine."
